About Us

Welcome to Indian Kitchen Stories! We are a set of talented and passionate foodies who love to feed our loved ones good food ,especially a variety of Tasty Indian Cuisine. We currently hold full time jobs , but felt that there is a dearth of information and knowledge about Indian Cuisine across the World. Indian Kitchen Stories started with a general conversation around how misunderstood the variety and depth of Indian food is outside India. We started this initiative with an Aim to introduce the vibrancy and taste of our Ancient Indian Food Wisdom with all of you through our Blogs, Recipes, Cooking videos and Events.
